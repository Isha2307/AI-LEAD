Priority Mapping

│ Score 80-100 → Priority: HOT     [Immediate Action]         │

│ Score 0-49   → Priority: COLD    [Nurture Track]            │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

Confidence Levels
─────────────────
❌ 0-39%:   Low confidence (insufficient signals)

✓  60-79%:  Good confidence (strong signals)
✅ 80-100%: High confidence (very clear signals)
